About plugin

characterCounter - First line of defence for spamming

Let your users know limitation about your message characters

V 1.0

This feather weight jQuery plugin is exceptionally simple to use and gives you power to customize few things. I wanted to go for one event, but could not make it so I used my Saturday to develop this. Funny, right?

$('ID/Class of Input box').characterCounter({
                        maxLen: 15,
                        warningLen: 5,
                        showMsg: true,
                        customMsg: "You have exceeded the permissible limit of characters..",
                        seprator: "/",
                        warningColor: "#d50000",
                                        });
